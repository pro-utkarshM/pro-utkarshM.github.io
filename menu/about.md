I am Utkarsh Maurya, an Electronics and Communication Engineering sophomore at NIT Hamirpur, passionate about community engagement. I have actively contributed as a Hyperleger Intern, Oculosence Intern, GeeksforGeeks Campus Ambassador, Startup Stash Contributor, Social Contributor (formerly Script Foundation), and SPEC NITH Executive. <br>

Parallel to my core studies and ideology, I extensively delved into Android Development, leveraging the Kotlin programming language to create innovative applications. Furthermore, my intellectual curiosity extends to the domains of Linux Kernel Development and Embedded Systems, where I am keenly interested in exploring the intricate interplay between hardware and software. <br>
